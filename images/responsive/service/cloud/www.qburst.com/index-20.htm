What We Do

Althea creates digital tools that empower rare disease communities interested in advancing research and drug development

Project Apollo

Althea is embarking on an exciting project that will usher in a new kind of drug development for rare diseases. Patient communities possess unique strengths that could revolutionize research and drug development. Unfortunately these strengths are extremely underutilized within the drug development ecosystem

As a result 95% of rare diseases do not have FDA approved drugs and the average price of a rare disease drug is $145K/patient/year. So just rearranging the deck chairs will not cut it. Althea is working on a patient powered digital ecosystem that will disrupt the current status quo.
