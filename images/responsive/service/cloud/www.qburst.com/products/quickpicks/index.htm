Quick Picks

Get more out of Salesforce. Quick Picks is a Salesforce app that lets you check courier status, news, stock prices, and other handy information without leaving the Salesforce portal. The application is free and can be a huge time saver for Salesforce users.

The Lightning-Ready Quick Picks

Go for the Lightning Experience on our new Quick Picks. Reimagined along the lines of mobile user experience, Lightning Experience drives efficiency and precision in sales. With our latest update, you can enjoy what you always loved about our Salesforce product with the additional benefits of superior user experience, performance, and productivity. The new version leverages Salesforce’s innovative technology, Lightning Component Framework.

Enhancements

  • Widgets for additional insights into Opportunities, Leads, and Accounts.
  • Components are Lightning App Builder-enabled to create easy custom pages.
  • Components can be directly and flexibly used to build mobile apps on Salesforce1.
  • Quick reference page for frequently used components.
  • Support for both Salesforce Classic and Lightning Experience.
  • Easy switching between Classic UI and Lightning Experience.

Quick Picks Widgets

Opportunity Insights

Have a quick view of the opportunity awaiting you.

Lead Insights

Quickly refer to the last action taken on your leads.

Account Insights

Keep track of opportunities open, won, or lost per account.

Currency Converter

Swiftly determine the value in Euros, Pounds, or any other currency.

Stock Indices

Dow Jones, NASDAQ, S&P—Track them all in one go.

Courier Tracker

Find out where your consignment is at this instant.

News Widget

Keep up with what's happening around the world.

Weather Tracker

Check the weather and plan meetings accordingly.

US Time Zone Clock

Never again disturb clients at unearthly hours.

Easy Configuration

Customize your installation with widgets and layout of your choice. On the Classic UI, you can display the widgets on the homepage. On the Lighting Experience UI, you can display them anywhere.

Compatibility

Quick Picks works with the Enterprise, Unlimited, Force.com, and Developer editions of Salesforce. The app requires browser cookies to be enabled and works best on Mozilla Firefox, Apple Safari, and Google Chrome browsers.
