About Us

OroSoft provides a one-stop software solutions bouquet to the Precious Metals Industry with an aim to help clients cut operating costs and increase profitability by automating their business processes. Being a specialized application designed by industry specialists, the purpose of these applications is to provide a suite of convenient and powerful tools for managing inventory, costing, sales, order processing, business intelligence, management reporting and more.

Neo Financials Trading Application

Specialised in designing and implementing ERP systems and solutions to facilitate end-to-end financial management work-flow and enable greater flexibility for the Precious Metals Industry, our aim is to be the trusted one-stop software solution provider to the Precious Metals Industry which also includes the Bullion Trading verticals, Financial Institutions, Refiners and Mining Companies.
