Smart Solution for
Data-Driven Manufacturing

SeeMyMachines brings to industrial manufacturing critical visibility spanning the entire business value chain, from management of demand to production. The capacity to track assets, people and processes in real time––
IoT’s claim to value––is critical in the light of growing competition and cost-cutting requirements of manufacturing establishments. SeeMyMachines gives you all of this capability, and as IoT enters the mainstream, makes you competitive for the new era of manufacturing.

  • Monitor Machine Condition

    Monitor Machine Condition

    The continuous volley of data supplied by sensors embedded in machines is analyzed in real time to ensure machines operate at the optimum level. Given this instant visibility into machine performance, operators can detect random failures before they snowball into a major problem that could derail plant operations and impact productivity.

  • Undertake Predictive Maintenance

    Undertake Predictive Maintenance

    Using forecasting models trained on data of past performance, SeeMyMachines provides prognostic information and early warnings on deteriorating machine parts. By predicting time-to-failure and provisioning maintenance services in advance, the severity of failure, machine downtime and cost of maintenance can be dramatically reduced.

  • Increase Plant Efficiency

    Increase Plant Efficiency

    SeeMyMachines brings visualised plant performance data to the plant manager’s fingertips via mobile devices, thereby improving the quality and speed of decision-making. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) tags along with networked sensors act as real-time location tracking systems. These help you identify the source of a problem accurately and take remedial action quickly.

  • Achieve Energy Efficiency

    Achieve Energy Efficiency

    Manufacturing is energy-intensive, but maintaining energy efficiency is a must to cap manufacturing overhead. SeeMyMachines gives you granular insight into electricity consumption in your plants, which you can draw on to implement energy-saving measures. You can optimise consumption by adjusting production or reducing the number of machines used, as the case may be.

  • Enable Connected Manufacturing

    Enable Connected Manufacturing

    On-demand manufacturing and custom products are key to success. A connected supply chain helps match manufacturing output with market demand. The visibility provided by SeeMyMachines down the supply chain goes a long way in supporting inventory management. Better integration of manufacturing processes with input suppliers leads to less wastage and costs.

  • Connect Factory Floor to Top Floor

    Connect Factory Floor to Top Floor

    Data is a critical manufacturing asset, but to become actionable, it needs to be integrated. SeeMyMachines supports a connected data ecosystem, linking your machines with Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems. With connected visibility into your operations, you can explore new opportunities for growth and plug loopholes that impede growth.

  • Transform Manufacturing Value Chain

    Transform Manufacturing Value Chain

    From monitoring the status of machines in real-world conditions to triggering alerts when machines are operated outside safe thresholds, SeeMyMachines allows Original Equipment Manufacturers to take service delivery to the next level. For dealers, it provides a unified platform to keep track of various machines supplied and streamline post-sales services.
