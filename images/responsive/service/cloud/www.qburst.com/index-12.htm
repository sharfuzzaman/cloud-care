Quick Poll

Recently there were uncorroborated reports in the social media of Chinese Air Force fighter aircraft having been moved to Skardu in POK. Simultaneously there were reports of military build up in Aksai Chin. The situation was developing into a threat to India from two fronts one front by China and another by Pakistan (actively supported by Chinese air and ground forces in POK). This collusive threat is becoming a reality and is no more a hypothetical scenario. India needs to prepare for such a threat and no government at the Centre can afford to ignore it.
